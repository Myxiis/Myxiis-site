About

Not an agency. Just someone who cares.

I'm Mattheu — designer, builder, and the person you'll actually be talking to.

I spent six years in the Air Force, where I learned how to travel light, solve problems fast, and take pride in getting the details right. That discipline is how I approach every site I build. Design and tech aren't a side hustle for me — they're what I'm genuinely obsessed with, and it shows in the work.

Most businesses get handed to a massive agency or a marketplace like Fiverr, where you're one of a thousand orders and a stranger cranks out something templated before moving on to the next. That's not what this is. Myxiis is personal. I work with local businesses one-on-one, learn what makes yours different, and build a site around it — then stick around as someone you actually know, not a support ticket. Fewer clients, real relationships, better work.
